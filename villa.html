Detached Villa

Size: Typically ranges between 300 to 700 square meters or more, ideal for large families.

From Rs.6,500/- onwards.

Fully seperated from other buildings.

Includes multiple rooms, private gardens, and often swimming pools.



Twin Villa

Size: Usually ranges between 250 to 400 square meters.

From Rs.4,500/- onwards.

Cheaper than detached villas due to shared walls.

Offers sufficient outdoor space.



Townhouse Villa

Size: Typically ranges between 150 to 300 square meters.

From Rs.8,500/- onwards.

Located in gated communities with security and maintenance service.

Ideal for small families or couples.



Cluster Villa

Size: Generally ranges between 200 to 400 square meters.

From Rs.8,500/- onwards.

Creates aesthetic hormony within the compound.

Encourages community interaction, ideal for families.
